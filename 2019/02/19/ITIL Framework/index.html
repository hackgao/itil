ITIL Framwork

在 2007年发布的ITIL V3中没有给出明确的流程数量，当时不同专家的文章中提到的ITIL V3流程数量并不一致，少的说 _20_ 个，多的说 _29_ 个。 到了2011年发布的ITIL V3:2011修订版中，明确写明26个流程。

Service Strategy (5)

  • Strategy Management for IT Services 战略制定
  • Service Portfolio Management 服务组合管理
  • Financial management IT服务财务管理
  • Demand Management 需求管理
  • Business relationship management (Introduced in ITIL 2011 Edition) 业务关系管理

Objective:

  • Identify strategy, services, customers
  • Exploit oppurtunities
  • Understanding assets

Service Design (8)

  • Design coordination (Introduced in ITIL 2011 Edition) 设计协作管理
  • Service Catalogue Management 服务目录管理
  • Service level Management 服务级别管理
  • Availability Management 可用性管理
  • Capacity Management 能力管理 / 容量管理
  • Information Security Management System 信息安全管理
  • Supplier Management 供应商管理
  • IT Service Continuity Management IT服务连续性管理

Objective:

  • Design effective services
  • design for current and furture needs
  • Minimise rework

Service Transition (7)

  • Transition planning and support 转换规划与支持
  • Change management 变更管理
  • Service asset and configuration management 资产与配置管理
  • Release and deployment management 发布与部属管理
  • Service validation and testing 服务验证与测试
  • (Change) Evaluation 服务评估

  • Knowledge management 知识管理

    Objective:

  • Plan and manage change

  • Manage service risk
  • Deploy services
  • Set expectations
  • Ensure value
  • Provide knowledge

Service Operation (5)

  • Event Management 事件管理
  • Incident Management 事故管理
  • Request Fulfillment 请求履行/请求实施
  • Problem Management 问题管理
  • Access Management 访问管理

4 Functions

  • Service Desk
  • Technical Management
  • Application Management
  • IT Operations Management

Objective:

  • Maintain business satisfaction
  • Manage outages
  • Maintain access to services

Continual Service Improvement (1)

  • 7 Step Improvement Process 7大改进流程

Service Reporting
Service Measurement

  1. Identify the strategy for improvement
  2. Define what you will measure
  3. Gather the data
  4. Process the data
  5. Analyse the information and data
  6. Present and use the information
  7. Implement improvement

Objective:

  • Improve services
  • Improve cost effectiveness
  • Meet changing business needs
  • Quality Management

CSF = Critical Success Factor
